About Me

Research Focus & Expertise

Building AI for Healthcare

I'm currently pursuing my Master's of Science by Research in Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad, where I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare.

My research focuses on medical imaging analysis, with particular emphasis on chest X-ray interpretation using Large Language Models (LLMs) and Vision-Language Models (VLMs) tailored for the Indian clinical context.

I collaborate with Nizam's Institute of Medical Sciences (NIMS) Hospital, working under the guidance of Prof. Bapi Raju S. (IIIT-H) and Dr. Tapabrata Chakraborty (UCL), developing AI solutions that bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and clinical practice.
